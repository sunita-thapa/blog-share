About BlogSphere

Our Story

BlogSphere started as a college assignment but grew into a fully-featured publishing platform. It's built with Flask, Bootstrap, and a genuine love of clean design.

Whether you're a developer sharing tutorials, a student documenting your journey, or a writer with stories to tell — BlogSphere gives you a home for your ideas.

Every account includes a personal portfolio page to showcase your projects, skills, and technologies to the world.
